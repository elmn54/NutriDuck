NutriDuck - Account & Data Deletion

⚠️ Important Notice: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

How to Delete Your NutriDuck Account

You can delete your NutriDuck account and all associated data directly from within the app. Follow these simple steps:

Step-by-Step Instructions:

Step 1: Open the NutriDuck app on your device
Step 2: Go to the "Profile" tab (bottom navigation)
Step 3: Scroll down to the "Account" section
Step 4: Tap on "Delete Account"
Step 5: Read the warning and confirm your decision by tapping "Delete"
Step 6: Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you delete your NutriDuck account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and profile data
  • Nutrition Data: All food logs, calorie tracking records, and nutrition goals
  • Activity Data: Exercise logs and activity tracking records
  • Photos: All food photos and images stored in the app
  • Settings: App preferences and customization settings
  • Subscription Data: Premium subscription information (cancellation will be handled separately)
  • Usage Analytics: All app usage data and analytics

Data Deletion Process

For Premium Users:

  • All data is deleted from our Firebase servers immediately
  • Cloud-synced nutrition and activity data is permanently removed
  • Account deletion is processed within 24 hours

For Free Users:

  • Local device data is deleted immediately
  • Account authentication data is removed from our servers
  • No data is retained after deletion

What Happens to Subscriptions?

  • Active subscriptions will be cancelled immediately
  • No refunds will be provided for remaining subscription periods
  • Billing information is handled by Google Play Store or App Store
  • You may need to cancel recurring billing separately through your app store

Data Retention Period

  • Immediate deletion: All user data is deleted immediately upon account deletion
  • No backup retention: We do not keep backups of deleted accounts
  • Legal compliance: No data is retained beyond what's legally required (none in this case)

Alternative: Partial Data Deletion

If you don't want to delete your entire account but want to remove specific data:

  • You can manually delete individual food logs and activities within the app
  • You can clear all data by logging out and then logging back in (for free users)
  • Contact us for assistance with partial data removal
